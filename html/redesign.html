"Responsive Redesign"

Redesigning Berk's Shoes and Clothing Site!


Overview:

I was tasked with redesigning and implementing a website, improving its design for usability, accessibility, and responsiveness! Our main assignment consisted of analyzing existing accessibility issues such as using the WAVE tool, building extensive prototypes, and ensuring that the website is clearly usable on all types of interfaces.

Original Webpage: Berk's Shoes & Clothing

I chose this website because I came across a number of glaring interface and usability issues, especially regarding product display and hierarchy. When I put the website through WAVE, there were 12 color contrast errors and alerts regarding no heading structures, and also very small text. I was surprised that there weren’t more contrast errors, but I realized it’s because images were used for text rather than text html tags. There was also no logical order in this gallery; there is no categorization in shoe or shirt pictures which makes it difficult for users to understand what the store sales offer, and the layout of the website was unintuitive because there is no hierarchy in the content of the page (sales are in the same carousel as the product images). Overall, the website does not make use of all web space (the border is too large) and the starry background made text extremely crowded and difficult to read.
